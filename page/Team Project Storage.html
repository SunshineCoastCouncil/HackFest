Team Project Storage

Once you have formed and registered your team you will be provided with login details for your Team's Project Storage space. This is where you will need to save your final entry. The storage space is a Windows share.

If you don't know how to map a network drive these are a couple of guides from the internet...

  • How to Map a Network Drive in Windows 10
  • Mount a Windows Share in OS X and Have it Reconnect at Login
Or hit up one of our HackFest helpers on the day.

You aren't required to use this space during the weekend for work in progress. But please check you can connect to it before you need to submit your entry.
