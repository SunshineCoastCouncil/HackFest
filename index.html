Welcome to HackFest 2016

Working in teams or individually, HackFest is for people with a mixture of skills including community members, entrepreneurs, developers, designers, digital media creators, artists, story tellers, researchers and open data enthusiasts, to hack - “take something and make it better” and create an outcome over an entire weekend.

Spark Bureau, 17 Ocean St, Maroochydore QLD
